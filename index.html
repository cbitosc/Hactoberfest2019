About Hacktoberfest

Hacktoberfest, the month-long festival of supporting and contributing to open-source, is back again this year. And, We are excited to share with you guys that CBIT will be hosting a 24-hour hackathon in our college as part of it, called CBIT Hacktoberfest Hackathon. Hacktoberfest is a virtual festival event to celebrate open source contributions presented by DigitalOcean and DEV. It is the easiest way to get into open source! We are hosting the Hacktoberfest in CBIT on the 25th and 26th of October 2019.
